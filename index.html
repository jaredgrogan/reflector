About Immersive Computational Aesthetics

Immersive Computational Aesthetics is an emerging interdisciplinary field that merges advanced web technologies, artificial intelligence, and artistic expression to create dynamic, interactive digital experiences. Our research pushes the boundaries of visual and auditory perception, exploring new paradigms in human-computer interaction and digital art creation.

Research Areas

Real-time Rendering

Advancing techniques for complex, interactive 3D environments

AI-driven Generative Art

Developing systems for creating evolving digital artworks

Multi-sensory Experiences

Integrating visual, auditory, and haptic elements in digital art

Data Visualization

Exploring the intersection of data, art, and interactivity
